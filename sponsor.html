How to Support

We need support in order to get our boat to the start line. If you are interested in helping out financially or want to learn more, we appreciate you already!!

We prefer to get to know our supporters personally, so please choose a time according to your convenience for a short 15-20 minute introduction. Alternatively, you can also reach out via email.

If you are looking for ways to donate to our chosen causes/charities, that information will soon be available on our Causes page. It's not up yet because we are still in the process of selecting charities.

Corporate Sponsorship

If your organization is interested in a corporate sponsorship, we'd be happy to talk further about how we can partner. We would likely offer a package along the following lines:
  • Your organization's name on the boat. Size/location depending on the sponsorship package.
  • On-land events and programming for members of your organization.
  • Shoutouts on our blog and social media channels!

Please let us know when you would be free for a quick meeting, or alternatively, reach out via email.
